COVID-FLU-RSV Comprehensive Viral Detection

The COVID-FLU-RSV Panel is a real-time PCR assay designed for the simultaneous qualitative detection of SARS-CoV-2, Influenza A, Influenza B, and Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV) viral RNA.

Each kit includes 100 tests.

This product is for research use only in the US.

  • SARS-CoV-2 - COVID-19 Virus Detection
  • Influenza A - Type A Flu Virus
  • Influenza B - Type B Flu Virus
  • RSV - Respiratory Syncytial Virus
  • Multiple Target Detection - Comprehensive Screening
  • High Sensitivity - Precise Viral RNA Identification
